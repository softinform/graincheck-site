Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Overview

GrainCheck ("the App") is a grain cost calculator and ledger for elevator operations. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

Your grain data — elevator profiles, ledger entries, calculation history, cultures, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or to any external server.

To measure which marketing channel led to an app install, the App uses the Tenjin analytics SDK, which collects and shares with Tenjin a limited set of technical data: your advertising ID, device identifiers (such as the Android App Set ID and a Tenjin installation ID), basic device information (model, operating system, language, and country derived from your IP address), and app install and session events. This data is pseudonymous and is not used to identify you personally.

Data Stored Locally

  • Elevator profiles (name, base indicators, rates)
  • Grain ledger entries (receipts, dispatches, balances)
  • Calculation history (input parameters, results)
  • Culture directory (names, prices)
  • App settings (language, country, theme, preferences)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses one third-party service: Tenjin (Tenjin, Inc.), a mobile measurement partner. It is used solely for install attribution and marketing analytics — to measure the performance of our advertising. The technical data listed above is shared with Tenjin, which may process it for these purposes. See Tenjin's privacy policy.

Opt-out: on Android, open Settings → Privacy → Ads and choose "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads personalization". The App then receives no usable advertising ID and your device is not attributed. To request deletion of data already collected, email hotline@softinform.com.ua.

The App uses no advertising networks and displays no ads.

Website Analytics & Advertising

This website (graincheck.app), which is separate from the App, uses cookies and tracking tools to measure our marketing: Google Analytics (Google), the Meta Pixel (Meta Platforms, Inc.), and Tenjin web tracking. They record page views, and when you tap an "Install" button you are routed through a Tenjin link (track.tenjin.com) that logs the click (device type and data derived from your IP) to attribute the resulting app install to the traffic source. The Meta Pixel stores a cookie (named _fbp) and shares page-view and click events with Meta for advertising measurement and optimization. This data is used only to measure and improve our advertising and is not used to identify you personally. You can block it via your browser's cookie settings or a tracking blocker. See the Meta, Google, and Tenjin privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional Pro subscription processed through Google Play or Apple App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by the respective platform. We do not have access to your payment information.

Permissions

On Android, the App requests the advertising ID permission (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) to enable the install attribution described above. The App does not request any sensitive permissions such as camera, precise location, contacts, or microphone.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: hotline@softinform.com.ua
